Welcome to the NXT Museum.

The first museum in the Netherlands dedicated entirely to media art. Explore large-scale, digital art installations that expand your perspective and sharpen the senses. The art of tomorrow, today.

History

Sound. Light. Movement. Our senses register changes in our environment before we consciously perceive them. Unconscious. Instinctive. No intention, no control. These are responses that trace back to how the human body is programmed. Art stimulates the senses, communicates ideas, emotions, and stories; shifts our gaze and brings us new perspectives.

In a former production studio spanning 2100 square meters, Nxt Museum adds a unique contribution to the flourishing creative district of Amsterdam-Noord. Inspired by the international museum landscape, where interactive, technology-driven exhibitions offer visitors a unique and often personalized experience, Nxt Museum aims to be a pioneer in the fields of emerging tech, new media art, and visitor experience in the Netherlands.

Nxt Museum, founded by the young entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen, showcases large-scale art installations developed through interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians, and scientists.
